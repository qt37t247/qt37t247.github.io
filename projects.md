# Projects & Code

Open‑source pipelines and R / Python packages from my research. Everything
below lives on [GitHub]({{ site.github_url }}).

<div class="projects">

  <div class="project">
    <h3><a href="https://github.com/qt37t247/ResDisMapper">ResDisMapper</a></h3>
    <p>
      R package for fine‑scale mapping of resistance to dispersal from genetic
      data. Companion to the <em>Molecular Ecology Resources</em> (2020) paper;
      produces raster‑based resistance surfaces that highlight landscape
      barriers to gene flow.
    </p>
    <span class="lang">R</span>
    <span class="meta">paper · tool</span>
  </div>

  <div class="project">
    <h3><a href="https://github.com/qt37t247/HabitatLossGenetics">HabitatLossGenetics</a></h3>
    <p>
      Spatially explicit, forward‑time simulation framework for the SLOSS
      (Single Large Or Several Small) debate, focused on genetic‑diversity
      outcomes under realistic, non‑equilibrium habitat‑loss scenarios.
    </p>
    <span class="lang">Python</span>
    <span class="meta">simulation</span>
  </div>

  <div class="project">
    <h3><a href="https://github.com/qt37t247/Horseshoe-crab-ddRAD">Horseshoe‑crab‑ddRAD</a></h3>
    <p>
      ddRAD‑Seq data‑processing and population‑genetics pipeline for three
      Asian horseshoe crab species. Underlies the 2021 <em>Evolutionary
      Applications</em> and 2025 <em>Conservation Letters</em> analyses.
    </p>
    <span class="lang">R</span>
    <span class="meta">pipeline</span>
  </div>

  <div class="project">
    <h3><a href="https://github.com/qt37t247/WGS-HPC-NUS">WGS‑HPC‑NUS</a></h3>
    <p>
      Whole‑genome‑sequencing bioinformatic pipeline developed for the NUS HPC
      cluster — alignment, variant calling, and downstream population‑genomic
      analyses, written as portable shell and SLURM scripts.
    </p>
    <span class="lang">Shell</span>
    <span class="meta">pipeline</span>
  </div>

  <div class="project">
    <h3><a href="https://github.com/qt37t247/CEAMEC">CEAMEC</a></h3>
    <p>
      Cost‑Effective Animal Management via Environmental Capacity — a decision
      framework (fork of the upstream tool) for allocating limited management
      budgets across candidate sites based on environmental carrying capacity.
    </p>
    <span class="lang">R</span>
    <span class="meta">fork · tool</span>
  </div>

  <div class="project">
    <h3><a href="https://github.com/qt37t247/timing_traps">timing_traps</a></h3>
    <p>
      Utilities for analysing time‑stamped trap data from field sampling
      campaigns.
    </p>
    <span class="meta">utility</span>
  </div>

</div>
